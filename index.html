Turnkey Project

Modern Structures will sign a Turnkey Contract under which the firm agrees to fully design, construct & equip Clients' Residential/Commercial/Industrial facility & turn the project over to the Client when it is ready for operation for remuneration. The client should be able to “Just Turn the Key.”
